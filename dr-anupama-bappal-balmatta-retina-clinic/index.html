Common Questions

How often should I get my eyes checked?

Adults should have a comprehensive eye exam every 1–2 years, or annually if you wear glasses.

Am I a candidate for LASIK?

Most people above 18 with stable vision are candidates. We perform a thorough pre-LASIK evaluation.

What are symptoms of glaucoma?

Glaucoma often has no early symptoms. Regular eye pressure checks are the best way to detect it early.

Is cataract surgery safe?

Yes, it is one of the most commonly performed and safest surgeries with very high success rates.
